How to Track your Food Order ?

Placed your order and looking for your order status? Don't worry, we have you covered and you can easily get updates on your food booking with us. Here're quick n easy steps to get updates on your order:

  • Enter Your Order ID: Once you place your food order with us, a unique Order ID is generated for every order and is sent to your registered mobile number that you had specified while placing your order. All you need is to quote the ID.
  • Enter Your Mobile Number: Just enter your registered mobile number. This is just for security check so that we can verify it in our databases for any updates related to your order.
  • Hit the "Track Order" button: Once you hit the submit button, the status of your food order would be displayed.

How the Order status is shown?

Once you enter your order id and mobile number, one can see a liner figure showcasing the status of the food order placed.

The Order Status updates can be-

  • Order Placed: Depicting that the order has been received and is awaiting for confirmation from restaurant's end.
  • Order Accepted: The restaurant to which one has placed the order has confirmed the order at their end.
  • Delivered: Order has been delivered.
